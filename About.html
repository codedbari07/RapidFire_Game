🔥 The Ultimate 50-Player Showdown

Rapid Fire drops 50 combatants into a relentless last-man-standing arena. No downtime, no RNG loot fiascos — just pure, unfiltered action. Matches last 12 minutes max, rewarding aggressive tactics, precision shooting, and environmental mastery. With over 10 million copies sold and a fiercely loyal community, Rapid Fire stands as a modern classic in the battle royale genre.

Our proprietary "Velocity Netcode" ensures buttery-smooth 50-player matches across PC, console, and mobile with ultra-low latency. Every victory feels earned, every defeat a lesson.

🚀 The Birth of a Battle Royale Legend

Launched in 2020 by Bari Games, Rapid Fire was born from a simple idea: strip away the boring parts of traditional royales. No camping meta, no inventory clutter — just raw combat. The "Velocity Engine" supports 50 real players per server with advanced tick rates, and cross-play unites gamers worldwide. What started as a small indie dream has exploded into a global phenomenon, praised for its arcade soul and competitive integrity.
